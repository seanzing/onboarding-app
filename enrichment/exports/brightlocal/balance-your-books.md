### Full Description
Balance Your Books LLC brings over 35 years of accounting experience offering comprehensive services including data entry, bank reconciliations, journal entries, inventory valuations, depreciation schedules, accounts payable, accounts receivable, adjusting journal entries, and preparation of financial statements. We specialize in month-end and year-end closing procedures. As a QuickBooks ProAdvisor, we are experts in QuickBooks and QuickBooks Point of Sale, with extensive experience in Timberline, MAS 90, PC Advisor, American Contractor, Micros/Aloha, and Compete. We are proud to be A+ rated with the Better Business Bureau.
