### Short Description

### Full Description
Highlander Chimney Sweeping & Masonry has been serving Northern Virginia since 1986. We are a family-owned business specializing in chimney sweeping, chimney inspection, chimney repair, fireplace installation, and masonry services. Our certified technicians are trained to identify and address any chimney or fireplace issues. We are CSIA certified and committed to providing safe, clean, and efficient chimney services. From routine cleaning to major repairs, we handle it all with professionalism and expertise. We serve Fairfax, Loudoun, Prince William, and surrounding counties.
